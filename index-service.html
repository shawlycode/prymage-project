TALLY SHEET MAGIC

Tally Sheet Magic is a reporting tool for Excel that will provide you with immediate business intelligence “out of the box”, enabling you to make critical decisions with confidence. Reports which are available in Tally, are in Tally’s standard formats and one might need the same report in User Modified Formats. As a result the entire report is retyped with all the needed details in Excel. This means duplication of work and is a huge waste of productive man-hours. Our Engineers have designed this solution for Tally users who need to generate MIS Reports at will. “Sheet Magic” integrates seamlessly with Excel and the user can construct his own reports using data from Tally, without any programming knowledge!
